About Nyatike Cyber Cafe

Serving the Nyatike community with reliable digital services

Our Story 🇰🇪

Nyatike Cyber Cafe was born from a simple observation: the people of Nyatike deserved access to the same digital services available in major towns — without having to travel long distances or pay exorbitant fees.

Located in the heart of Nyatike Town, Migori County, we opened our doors to bridge the digital gap in our community. From students applying for university to farmers checking government subsidies, from job seekers crafting CVs to families applying for passports — we serve everyone with the same care and dedication.

We believe that access to technology is not a luxury but a necessity. That's why we keep our prices affordable and our service top-notch. When you walk into Nyatike Cyber Cafe, you're not just a customer — you're family.

Our team is trained to assist with everything from simple printing to complex government portal applications. We stay updated on the latest changes to KRA, eCitizen, and other platforms so you don't have to figure it out alone.
