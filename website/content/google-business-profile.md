## FAQ Answers

### Q: What services does Aego Cyber Cafe offer?
A: We offer government services (KRA, eCitizen, NHIF, NSSF, NTSA, HELB), professional document services (CVs, business plans, letters), design services (flyers, cards, posters, logos), printing, scanning, and internet access. We also offer remote services via WhatsApp for clients who can't visit in person.

### Q: How much does it cost to file KRA returns at Aego Cyber Cafe?
A: KRA returns filing starts at KES 500 for employment income and KES 800 for business income. KRA PIN registration is KES 300. All prices include our service fee. Government fees are additional where applicable.

### Q: Can I get services done remotely without visiting the cafe?
A: Yes! We offer full remote services. WhatsApp us with what you need, pay via M-Pesa, and we handle everything. We deliver documents via WhatsApp or Email. This is popular with Kenyans in the diaspora and busy professionals.

### Q: Do you write CVs and how much does it cost?
A: Yes, we write professional CVs using AI-enhanced tools for KES 800. A CV + Cover Letter bundle is KES 1,200. Same-day turnaround available. Our CVs are tailored to your target job and optimized for applicant tracking systems.

### Q: What are your payment methods?
A: We accept M-Pesa (preferred), cash, and bank transfer for larger orders. M-Pesa provides instant confirmation and a clear transaction record.

### Q: Where is Aego Cyber Cafe located?
A: We are located in Nyatike, Migori County, Kenya. We serve clients from across Migori County and remotely serve Kenyans worldwide.

### Q: Do you help with business registration?
A: Yes. We assist with business name registration (sole proprietorship), limited company registration, partnership deeds, and all related documentation. Business name registration starts at KES 1,000 plus government fees.

### Q: Can Kenyans in the diaspora use your services?
A: Absolutely. We serve many Kenyans abroad — in the US, UK, Canada, Australia, and the Middle East. Just WhatsApp us, pay via M-Pesa, and we handle your Kenyan paperwork. Popular services include KRA returns, birth certificates, and business registration.

### Q: How long does it take to get a CV done?
A: Most CVs are completed same-day — often within a few hours. If you need it urgently, let us know and we'll prioritize your order.

### Q: Is my personal information safe with you?
A: Yes. We take confidentiality seriously. All documents are handled securely, and we never share your personal information with third parties. M-Pesa transactions provide a clear payment trail for your records.
