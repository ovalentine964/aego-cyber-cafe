## What Is eCitizen?

eCitizen (ecitizen.go.ke) is Kenya's one-stop online platform for government services. Launched to digitize public services, it connects citizens to multiple government agencies through a single portal. In 2026, the platform handles millions of transactions monthly.

In Kiswahili, they call it *huduma kwa njia ya mtandao* — services through the internet. And that's exactly what it is.

## How to Register on eCitizen

### Step 1: Create an Account
1. Go to **ecitizen.go.ke**
2. Click **"Create Account"**
3. Enter your national ID number and name
4. Create a password
5. Verify your email address

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile
- Add your phone number
- Upload a passport photo
- Verify your details

### Step 3: Access Services
Once registered, you can browse all available services, pay via M-Pesa, and track your applications.

## How to Pay for eCitizen Services

eCitizen payments are made via **M-Pesa Paybill 206206**. Here's how:

1. Go to M-Pesa on your phone
2. Select **Lipa na M-Pesa** → **Pay Bill**
3. Enter Business Number: **206206**
4. Enter Account Number: **your eCitizen reference number**
5. Enter the amount
6. Confirm and pay

The payment reflects on eCitizen within minutes.
