## Common Questions from Diaspora Clients

**Q: Can you really do KRA returns without me being there?**
A: Yes. We file on iTax using your details. You send us your KRA PIN and password (securely), and we handle the rest. Thousands of Kenyans in the diaspora file this way.

**Q: How do I send you my documents?**
A: WhatsApp is easiest. Photos of your ID, certificates, or any documents — just snap and send. We can also receive via email.

**Q: Is M-Pesa safe for international payments?**
A: M-Pesa is one of the most secure payment platforms in Africa. Every transaction has a confirmation code. If you have M-Pesa (Safaricom), you can pay from anywhere. If not, you can use M-Pesa Global or have someone in Kenya pay on your behalf.

**Q: What if I need something done urgently?**
A: Tell us. We handle urgent requests regularly. Some services can be completed same-day if you message us early enough (Kenya time, EAT).

**Q: Can I trust you with my personal information?**
A: We understand the concern. We've built our reputation on trust. We can provide references from other diaspora clients, and all our processes are documented and transparent.

**Q: What if the government service takes longer than expected?**
A: We'll keep you updated. Some services (like police clearance or logbook transfers) have government timelines we can't control. But we'll follow up relentlessly and keep you informed.
