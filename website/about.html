My Story

I started Aego Cyber Cafe because I saw a gap in my community. In Nyatike, people were traveling long distances to get basic digital services — filing KRA returns, printing documents, applying for government services. Many didn't know how to use online platforms. Others were being overcharged.

As an Economics & Statistics student, I understood how these systems work. I also understood the barriers — cost, distance, digital literacy — that kept people in Nyatike from accessing the services they needed.

So I opened a cyber cafe. But not just any cyber cafe. I wanted to build something that uses the latest technology to serve people better, faster, and more affordably. That's where AI comes in.

Why AI?

AI isn't just a buzzword for us. It's a practical tool that helps us:

  • Write better CVs — AI helps us craft professional, ATS-friendly CVs that actually get interviews
  • Reduce errors — government forms require precision. AI helps us catch mistakes before submission
  • Work faster — what used to take hours now takes minutes, meaning lower prices for you
  • Serve remotely — we can handle your documents from anywhere, and deliver via WhatsApp

I believe AI should work for everyone — not just people in Nairobi or abroad. That's why I've made it the backbone of our services here in Nyatike.

Our Mission

To bridge the digital divide in Nyatike and Migori County.

Everyone deserves access to digital services — whether it's filing taxes, writing a CV, registering a business, or applying for a passport. We make it possible, affordable, and convenient.
