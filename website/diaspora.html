Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay from abroad?

M-Pesa works internationally via the M-Pesa app, Safaricom app, or through M-Pesa global. You can also use WorldRemit or similar services that send to M-Pesa. We'll guide you if needed.

How do I send you my documents?

WhatsApp is easiest — just send photos or scans of your documents. We can also receive via email. We'll tell you exactly what we need for each service.

How will I receive my completed documents?

We send everything via WhatsApp (PDF or photos) and/or email. For physical documents (like printed certificates), we can arrange courier delivery at additional cost.

Is my personal information safe?

Absolutely. We handle all documents with strict confidentiality. Your information is never shared with third parties. We delete sensitive files after service completion.

What if I need a service not listed?

WhatsApp us! We handle all kinds of government and document services. If we can do it, we will. If not, we'll point you in the right direction.
