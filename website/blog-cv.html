How to Write a CV That Gets You Hired in Kenya

Your CV is your first impression. Here's how to make it count — 2026 edition.

📅 January 2026 · 6 min read

Why Most CVs Get Rejected

Recruiters spend an average of 6 seconds scanning a CV. If yours doesn't grab attention immediately, it goes to the "no" pile. Common mistakes include:

  • ❌ Too long (3+ pages for entry-level positions)
  • ❌ Generic — same CV for every job application
  • ❌ Poor formatting — hard to read, messy layout
  • ❌ Spelling and grammar errors
  • ❌ No quantified achievements — just listing duties

What Makes a Great CV

  • ✅ 1–2 pages maximum — concise and focused
  • ✅ Tailored to the job — match keywords from the job ad
  • ✅ Clean formatting — professional, easy to scan
  • ✅ Achievements, not duties — "Increased sales by 30%" not "Responsible for sales"
  • ✅ ATS-friendly — many companies use software to scan CVs
  • ✅ No errors — zero typos, perfect grammar

CV Structure for Kenyan Job Market

  1. Header: Name, phone, email, location
  2. Professional Summary: 2–3 sentences about you
  3. Work Experience: Most recent first, with achievements
  4. Education: Degrees, certifications, relevant courses
  5. Skills: Both technical and soft skills
  6. References: "Available on request" is fine
